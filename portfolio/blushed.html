Blushed

Blushed is a Chat Stories app

Client

Magnum Soln.

Service

Chat Stories

Date

November 11, 2018

Challenge

Magnum Solutions from Banagalore came up with a unique app idea. They wanted an app which allowed users to enjoy stories in the form of chat. The app would show messages between two people. User taps on the screen and the app reveals messages one by one.

Solution

We developed an easy to use Chat Stories app. The major task here was to paginate the messages. A chat story may have 1k-2k messages. We needed to fetch only a few at once. Once they are read, fetch the next.

This was a project we developed from scratch during an internship. The app UI has evolved after that. But still the core logic is written by us. The new app looks as follows.

Features

  • Top Stories

    View the latest and popular stories on top

  • Categries

    Browse through various categories

  • Dark Mode

    Read in bed without hurting your eyes

  • Filter

    Filter stories based on language you prefer

  • Autoplay

    Messages can be shown in an hands-free autoplay mode

  • Unique options panel

    From options, one can toggle sound effects, read description of story or delete progress

  • Likes & Bookmarks

    Like & bookmark your favourite stories

  • Guide

    The app helps you understand the UI/UX

  • Track progress

    Easily see how much you've read

  • Shimmer effect

    Beautiful Shimmer animation for loading data

  • Swipe to remove

    Easily remove stories from Bookmarks, Likes by swiping

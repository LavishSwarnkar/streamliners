Karobar Easy

‘Karobar Easy’ app helps people manage their businesses digitally and effectively. With features like staff attendance, salary calculator, employee tracker, monthly report and many more, the app is designed to make people's everyday business easy.

Client

Logocial

Service

Business management made easy

Date

May 11, 2021

Challenge

Existing business management apps like PagarBook, PagarKhata put some restrictions on businesses. In the real world, attendance management isn't that easy. One has to take care of many test cases. Existing apps paid the cost of missing these cases and simplified their apps. Businesses needed something easy yet powerful management app.

Solution

We thought of the problem from a different perspective. We developed the Karobar app which provides much more dynamism to the businesses when it comes to attendance management. Additionally, we provide staff location tracking features.

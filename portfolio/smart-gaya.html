Smart Gaya

Smart Gaya app helps Gaya-wasis order services, general store items, get News and a lot more

Client

Personal

Service

Local service listing, E-Com

Date

January 1, 0001

Challenge

If you are not able to implement what you learnt then you are just wasting your time. One of our student observed some problems in his town (Gaya, Bihar) and wanted to make E-Com app. Also, he wanted to bring every service online so that people can reached the right place at the right time in the COVID crisis

Solution

This app provides a platform for the local Gayawis where they can get access to the daily need services such as the contact details of Plumbers, Electricians, AC mechanics, etc. The services of the Smart Gaya App don't stop there. It also brings you an online ration store from where you can order daily groceries and get the delivery at your doorsteps.

User app

Following are the major features of the user app :

  • All service at your fingertips

    You can order any service you want, from salon, carpenter, AC Services, WiFi provider, Painters, Tutor, RO service, Plumber, CCTV, Driver, Accounts and many more

  • Gaya Tour

    It helps users find cool and chilling Place from temples to bars. So one gets to know about most important places of Gaya

  • General Store

    This is used by locals to Order General Store items according to their needs which gets delivered at their doorsteps at the EOD

  • Drawer full of options

    In this app, hamburger icon provider you many useful options like Order History, Connect, Rate, Achievement etc.

Achievements

50+ orders delivered worth Rs. 60,000+

(As of April 21, 2021)

The SMART GAYA app was selected amongst one of the few start-ups of the Start-up Bihar competition, and was considered for Incubation.

NAAD Pricing Plan

High Standards Protect you from Low Quality Experiences.

Only Videos won’t work

We have seen people learning App Development through videos. And it’s terrible, they get stuck in between, no one to help, no one to give feedback and improve what they have built. And they give up ultimately. Learning that way is difficult. Why? Because videos can’t cover everything. You learn through guidance, by practicing and building real world products. Also, you have to keep learning.

Our uniqueness

Now, what do we do differently? Well, here are the benefits of joining our community and learning here :

  • You get challenging and handcrafted tasks
  • You get feedback and suggestions on improving your submitted task solutions
  • LIVE doubt clearing sessions once / twice a week
  • MOST IMPORTANT : You will get real world projects from us. And we will help you design the application from scratch. You will get full assistance in building the application. And it’s our guarantee that if you are willing, we will help you become one of the best software engineers out there. We will guide you along your way of becoming a great freelancer and Software Engineer.

Great things come at a cost

If you want to learn alone, it’s perfectly fine. Video sessions will be available for FREE to all. But if you want the above benefits, it comes at a cost. Don’t worry you need not pay us now! First learn and build something i.e. get the above benefits, work with us on great real world projects, build products and then pay for the course from your earnings. In a nutshell, you won’t be paid for your first few projects with us until your earning sums up to Rs. 5000. (This is PLAN A and you have to earn it based on your efforts)

Best one gets it FREE

Based on your efforts, we will pick the best learner (only one) and (S)he gets the course for FREE. No need to pay a single paisa!

Scholarships

If you work hard, with full dedication, complete tasks on time, put extra efforts, help others i.e. if you are a good learner then the course cost gets waived off for you!

  • 2nd & 3rd student will get 40% off ( need to pay only ₹3000 )
  • 4th & 5th will get 20% off ( need to pay only ₹4000 )
  • Remaining students need to pay ₹5000 after earning from projects except

Plan B

  • Our course is organised in the form of modules. After a first few modules, you have to pay Rs. 2000 and at the end of the course Rs. 3000.
  • By default everyone will be in Plan B. But based on our observation on your efforts, we will shift people into Plan A. So, they need not pay directly. They will pay after they earn on completing projects.
  • Someone who doesn’t work hard, will always be in Plan B. So, they can’t continue without direct payment.

The Critical Stage

One who is found in any of the following situations (but not limited to) is counted in Critical / Vulnerable stage. (S)he may even get kicked out in rare circumstances.

  • Not completing assignments on time
  • Not attending weekly meetings
  • Not being interactive / answering questions in weekly meetings
  • Always looking for reasons to delay tasks
  • If you ever land on critical stage, you will have to pay complete fees Rs. 5000 before you proceed any further.

This is truly different

Software Engineering isn’t something that can be learnt in 1-2 months. It’s a constantly learning & growing process. We are trying something new. Today, the traditional way to Software Engineering starts with Competitive programming. But we argue that. We think (Good programming, Critical Thinking, Logical Thinking, Basic DSA, Never Give up attitude, Keep learning, Learn when required, Teamwork, communication skills) is what is required to become a SE. You need not have the best ranks on coding platforms to become one. We are an example in front of you. We are not competitive programmers, but have built many products including some large scale ones. If we can do it, anyone full of energy, willingness and dedication can do it.

Final thoughts

A great person once said “Pehle sikh lo, fir kamaana”. And we believe in that. As a college student, your motive shouldn’t be earning money. Instead it must be learning and building skills. This is the time you invest in yourself. If you are doing something great, you need not worry about money, it will naturally follow. Also, there is no financial expectations from your parents while in college. So, enjoy the learning process.

Then why are we charging? We are almost out of college and that brings new expectations from parents that we can earn. It’s not that we are unemployable. We can easily get a good job. But No, we chose a different path to benefit you all. Since childhood we came hearing :

बड़ा भया तो क्या भया, जैसे पेड़ खजूर ।
पंथी को छाया नहीं फल लागे अति दूर ।

So, we can’t be selfish, we can’t prepare for a job and leave you in the darkness. We want you to be independent, grow your skills and become a job creator rather than a job seeker.
